Veer Seva Food Foundation - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Veer Sewa Food Foundation ("the Foundation") collects, uses, and protects the personal information provided by users of its website and services.

1. Information Collected:

  • The Foundation may collect personal information, including but not limited to names, email addresses, and contact details, when voluntarily submitted by users through forms or other means.

2. Use of Information:

  • Personal information collected by the Foundation may be used to communicate with users, process donations, and provide information about the Foundation's activities.
  • The Foundation will not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties without the user's consent, except as required by law.

3. Security:

  • The Foundation takes reasonable measures to protect the security and confidentiality of users' personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Cookies:

  • The Foundation's website may use cookies to enhance the user experience. Users can choose to disable cookies in their browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of the website.

5. Third-Party Links:

  • The Foundation's website may contain links to third-party websites. The Foundation is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

6. Updates to Privacy Policy:

  • The Foundation reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically for any changes.
