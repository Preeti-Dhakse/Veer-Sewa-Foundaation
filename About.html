About Us

Welcome to Veer Sewa Food Foundation, where our mission is to make a positive impact on the community through our commitment to providing nutritious meals to those in need. Our foundation believes in the power of unity and service to create a better, healthier world. Veer Sewa Food Foundation is a compassionate beacon committed to alleviating hunger and malnutrition within our communities. Guided by the principles of selfless service (sewa) and valor (veer), our foundation operates with the steadfast mission of ensuring that no individual in our society faces the pangs of hunger. Through dedicated initiatives, we extend a helping hand to the underprivileged, delivering not only nutritious meals but also fostering a sense of community and support.


At Veer Sewa Food Foundation, we are dedicated to addressing the issue of food insecurity by distributing free meals to individuals and families facing hunger. Our team of volunteers works tirelessly to ensure that no one goes to bed hungry. Our core objectives center around impactful food distribution efforts, reaching marginalized communities, orphanages, and shelters. By organizing regular food drives, we strive to make wholesome meals accessible to all, transcending socio-economic barriers. At Veer Sewa Food Foundation, we recognize the power of education and empowerment in breaking the cycle of poverty. Consequently, we actively support educational programs and skill development initiatives to uplift and empower individuals within the community.


To be a part of Veer Sewa Food Foundation is to embrace a call to action. We invite individuals, businesses, and volunteers to join us in this noble cause. Whether through volunteering time, contributing resources, or exploring partnerships, your involvement can be the catalyst for transformative change. Let us unite in our mission to serve with valor, compassion, and the unwavering belief that together, we can build a world where no one goes hungry.
